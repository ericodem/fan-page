"The Tour Of Walla Walla Bicycle Race

Live To Ride

The tour of Walla Walla is a 4 day stage race that rides through the rolling hills and beautiful vineyards of the walla walla valley. In between races spend some time relaxing at one of our 134 local winerys

I have ridden in this amazing event 8 times now and every year it is the highlight of my racing season. Come join me!!
